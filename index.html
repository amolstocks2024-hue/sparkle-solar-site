FAQs

How does the MNRE rooftop subsidy work?

We handle the application, vendor empanelment, inspection and disbursement steps for you.

Which is best: On-grid, Off-grid or Hybrid?

On-grid for lower upfront & net-metering; Hybrid for savings + backup; Off-grid for remote/no net-metering.

How much roof area is required?

Approx 80–100 sq.ft per kW depending on panel efficiency and shade.

What maintenance is needed?

Periodic cleaning and basic checks; we provide support and guidance.
